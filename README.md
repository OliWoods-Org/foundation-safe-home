> **Lead exposure causes irreversible brain damage in children.** Low-income renters in pre-1978 housing are most exposed. Inspections cost $300-500 and landlords resist. Asbestos, mold, and radon compound the problem. This is a complete market failure — no AI tools exist.

## The Solution

Risk-score any US address for lead, asbestos, radon, and mold probability. Connect tenants with free testing programs. Generate legal demand letters. Track children's blood lead levels.

## Agents

**RiskScorer** — Predict hazard probability from property age, location, code violations, demographics
**TenantRights** — Landlord obligations and legal remedies by jurisdiction
**TestingFinder** — Free/low-cost testing programs and abatement resources
**PhotoAnalyzer** — Preliminary hazard identification from photos (peeling paint, visible mold)
**ChildHealthTracker** — Blood lead level tracking and doctor referral

## Safety

MANDATORY: If a child shows symptoms of lead poisoning, seek immediate medical attention.

## Data Sources

Census AHS, HUD lead data, CDC childhood lead surveillance, state code violation databases, EPA radon zones
